Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Any high schooler can participate! If you're starting ninth grade in the fall, you can participate too! However, just because you are not a high schooler doesnt mean you can't help out. If you have a passion for CS and are stilled interested in coming, please sign up to be a mentor or teach a workshop!

What can I make?

Anything you'd like. Come up with your favorite idea and make it reality! Use any programming languages, development stacks, APIs, and other tools you need. If you cant think of any ideas or you need some inspiration check out DevIdeas and continue on from there.

What if I donâ€™t know how to code?

First of all, thats awesome! We will have mentors and workshops available to help you for the entire duration of the event! All we ask from you is the willingness to learn and to create something you can be proud of.

How much does this cost?

Short answer: nothing. Long answer: $0.00. We will provide you with resources, tools, mentors, and food all for free. All we need from you is that you have been ready to learn and have fun!

What should I bring to the hackathon?

A laptop + charger
A cellphone
Sleeping bag (highly recommended)
At least two sets of clothes
Basic toiletries

Do I need to be at the venue at all times?

Due to security reasons, you must stay in the building at all times. If you have an emergency or really have to leave, don't worry. We'll work it out.

What's the max team size?

The maximum number of people in a team will be four. However, if you would like to go solo or work with fewer people, that works too!

I don't have a team!

We'll have a team building session right before hacking starts. You'll quickly introduce yourselves and form teams however you choose. You'll make cool new friends!

I have more questions!!!

If you have any questions that we have not addressed, please don't hesitate to contact us at contact@dchacks.org
